# Harmonie Governance Framework – Zambezi Basin Council

## Article 1: Authority
The Zambezi Basin Council (ZBC) replaces the Zambezi River Authority. All water allocation requires 80% multi-level approval.

## Article 2: Council Structure
- **Level 1**: Upstream farmers (30% seats)
- **Level 2**: Mid-basin towns (20% seats)
- **Level 3**: Kariba-adjacent cities (20% seats)
- **Level 4**: Downstream communities (30% seats + ecological veto)

## Article 3: Problem-Victim Priority
30-40% of seats reserved for communities directly harmed by water decisions.

## Article 4: APO AI Enforcement
All votes recorded on blockchain. APO AI auto-triggers Level 4 veto if ecological flow &lt; 200 m³/s.

## Article 5: Non-Spontaneous Selection
Council members peer-nominated, not self-selected. Dark Triad screening mandatory.
